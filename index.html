IPD-Net: SO(3) Invariant Primitive Decompositional Network for 3D Point Clouds
Ramesh Ashok Tabib, Nitishkumar Upasi, Tejas Anvekar, Dikshit Hegde, Uma Mudenagudi

We present a framework inspired by human cognition to decompose point clouds into four primitive 3D shapes (plane, cylinder, cone, and sphere) and enable machines to understand the objects irrespective of its orientations. We demonstrate the results of our proposed methodology for SO(3) invariant decomposition on TraceParts Dataset, and show the generalizability of proposed IPD-Net as plugin for downstream task on classification of point clouds.
